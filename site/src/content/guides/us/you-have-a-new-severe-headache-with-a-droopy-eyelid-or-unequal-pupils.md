# What to do if… you have a new severe headache with a droopy eyelid or unequal pupils (USA)

## Short answer
Call 911 now. A new severe headache with a droopy eyelid or noticeably unequal pupils needs emergency evaluation.

## Do not do these things
- Do not drive yourself to the ER if 911 is available; use an ambulance.
- Do not wait to see if it improves, even if symptoms come and go.
- Do not take aspirin, extra pain medicine, or someone else’s medicine unless 911 dispatch or a clinician tells you to.
- Do not drink alcohol or use recreational drugs to blunt the pain.
- Do not ignore sudden weakness, numbness, face drooping, speech trouble, confusion, trouble walking, or major vision change.

## What to do now
1. **Call 911 and say:** “I have a new severe headache with a droopy eyelid or unequal pupils.”
2. **Say when it started:** give the time the headache began, the time you first noticed the eyelid or pupil change, and the last time you were definitely normal.
3. **Tell 911 about any stroke-like symptoms. Do not delay the call to check for these:**
   - Face drooping
   - Arm weakness or numbness
   - Speech difficulty
   - Sudden vision change
   - Sudden trouble walking, dizziness, or loss of balance
4. **Get into a safer waiting position:** sit or lie down somewhere responders can reach you easily. If you feel faint or vomit, lie on your side.
5. **Make entry easier if you can do it safely:** unlock the door, turn on a light, and keep pets away from the entrance.
6. **Gather only essentials within reach:** ID, insurance card if you have one, a medication list or medication bottles, allergies, and major medical conditions. Mention blood thinners or recent head, neck, or eye injury.
7. **Do not eat or drink unless 911 dispatch or a clinician tells you to.** If you have essential prescribed medicine due now, tell dispatch or the responding clinicians before taking or skipping it.

## What can wait
- You do not need to decide whether this is a migraine, cluster headache, stroke, aneurysm, eye problem, or something else.
- You do not need to call your primary care clinician before calling 911 for this symptom combination.
- You do not need to pack a bag, search online, or arrange your own transport.

## Important reassurance
Calling 911 for this is appropriate. Many headaches are not dangerous, but a new severe headache with a new droopy eyelid or unequal pupils is not something you should sort out alone or wait through.

## Scope note
These are first steps only to reduce risk and get emergency assessment started. Later decisions about diagnosis, tests, treatment, or follow-up may need specialist medical help.

## Important note
This is general information, not medical advice, a diagnosis, or professional medical care. If these symptoms are happening now, returning, or worsening, use 911 or emergency medical care.
