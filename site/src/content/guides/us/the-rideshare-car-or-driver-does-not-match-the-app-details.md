# What to do if… the rideshare car or driver does not match the app details (USA)

## Short answer
Do not get in. Move away from the car to a safer nearby place. Once you are safer, cancel the trip in the app and request another ride; call 911 immediately if you need emergency assistance, such as if you are threatened, followed, or blocked from leaving.

## Do not do these things
- Do not get in to sort out the mismatch or because someone insists it is fine.
- Do not accept an off-app ride, including a cash ride or a request to cancel and travel anyway.
- Do not move closer to the window to debate the details.
- Do not provide a pickup PIN until you have confirmed the matched vehicle and driver and are ready to start the correct ride.
- Do not let rain, lateness, surge pricing, or pressure override a mismatch.
- Do not delay moving away in order to take photos or gather details.

## What to do now
1. **Create space.** Step away from the car. Move inside a business, near venue or airport staff, near security, or toward other people if that is available.
2. **Check the app details from a distance.** Compare the license plate, vehicle make and model, vehicle color if shown, and driver photo. If the plate or vehicle does not match, or the driver photo appears not to match, do not get in.
3. **Cancel once you are away from the car.** Use the closest available safety-related or mismatch reason in the app. Any fee question can wait until you are safer.
4. **Call 911 if you need immediate emergency assistance.** Call if you are threatened, followed, blocked from leaving, or otherwise need immediate help from police, fire, or ambulance services.
5. **Save details only if it is safe.** After moving away, take a screenshot of the trip details or note the arriving car's plate and your location if doing so does not slow you down or draw attention.
6. **Report the mismatch after reaching a safer place.** Use the app's help or safety route and provide the details it requests.
7. **Arrange another pickup from a safer point.** At an airport or venue, use the official rideshare pickup area or a staffed entrance. Elsewhere, wait inside or near other people rather than beside the curb.
8. **If you noticed the mismatch after getting in:** if it feels safe to do so, ask to end the ride at a busy public place and get out. If you feel trapped, the driver refuses to stop, or you need immediate emergency assistance, call 911.

## What can wait
- You do not need to decide whether the mismatch was a mistake or intentional.
- You do not need to confront the driver or prove that the details are wrong.
- You do not need to resolve a fee, refund, rating, or detailed report while you are still exposed at the pickup point.
- You do not need to decide whether to make a non-emergency law-enforcement report right now.

## Important reassurance
It is reasonable to decline a ride when the app details do not match. You do not owe anyone an explanation before moving away.

## Scope note
These are first steps for reaching a safer pause. Platform follow-up, fee questions, and any non-emergency reporting decisions can wait until you are safer and may need support from the rideshare service or an appropriate specialist.

## Important note
This is general information, not legal, medical, financial, therapeutic, or other professional advice. Call 911 immediately if you need emergency assistance.
