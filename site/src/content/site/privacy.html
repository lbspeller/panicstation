PanicStation.org does not require an account and is designed to be usable without collecting personal information as part of normal browsing.

What is stored in your browser

To make the site work smoothly, we use your browser’s built-in storage:

  • Local storage: used to remember your jurisdiction preference (UK/USA) and your category filter (if you choose one).
  • Session storage: used to remember short-lived interface state, such as whether “results mode” is active for the current tab/session.

This storage stays in your browser. It is not automatically sent to us as personal data.

Cookies

At present, the site does not need traditional cookies for core functionality. If we later add analytics or advertising, this section will be updated and you may be shown a consent prompt where required.

Search behaviour

Search runs in your browser against a prebuilt index of guides. Your search terms are not intended to be sent to a server as part of normal operation (other than standard web hosting logs, described below).

Server logs (standard web hosting)

Like most websites, the hosting and delivery infrastructure may automatically process basic technical information when you load pages, such as:

  • IP address (often temporarily)
  • device/browser details (user agent)
  • date/time of requests
  • requested URLs and response status codes

This is typically used for security, debugging, and performance (for example, blocking abuse or diagnosing outages). Exact retention and detail depend on the hosting provider.

Third-party services

If third-party services are added (for example, analytics or advertising), they may set cookies or use similar identifiers. If this happens, PanicStation.org will:

  • describe what is used and why
  • provide a way to manage consent where required
  • limit non-essential tracking where possible

Your choices

  • You can clear your saved preferences at any time by clearing site data in your browser (cookies/site storage).
  • You can switch jurisdiction manually using the UK/USA toggle.
  • You can choose not to use category filters.

Contact

If you need clarification about privacy on this site, use the site’s contact method once it is published (this section can be updated with a dedicated email address when available).
