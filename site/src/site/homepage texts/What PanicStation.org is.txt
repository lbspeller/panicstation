## What PanicStation.org is

PanicStation.org is a plain-English guide library for stressful moments when something has happened and you are not sure what to do next.

Each guide is designed to help you pause, avoid common mistakes, and find a practical route forward. Searching is usually the quickest way to find the right guide, especially if you describe the situation in your own words.

If you would rather scan the library, you can browse all guides and then choose the country and category that apply.
