## Corrections, contact and privacy

If you notice an error, outdated information, unclear wording, or a broken link, you can report it by email.

Correction reports help keep PanicStation.org guides clearer and more useful. Where possible, please include the guide title, page URL, and a short explanation of the issue.

PanicStation.org does not have user accounts, comments, or contact forms. Search is handled locally in your browser. The Privacy Policy explains how cookies, advertising, Cloudflare, and email contact are handled.
