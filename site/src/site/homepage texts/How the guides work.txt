## How the guides work

PanicStation.org guides are built to be quick to scan when you are stressed, short on time, or unsure what matters first.

Most guides follow the same basic pattern:

- **Short answer:** the immediate priority in plain language.
- **Do not do these things:** common mistakes that could make the situation harder to fix.
- **What to do now:** practical first steps while the issue is active.
- **What can wait:** tasks to leave until the immediate pressure has passed.
- **Important notes and resources:** limits, context, and official or specialist links where available.
