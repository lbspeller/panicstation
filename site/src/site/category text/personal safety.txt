Browse PanicStation.org guides for stressful situations involving personal safety, threats, intimidation, harassment, being followed, unsafe contact, or uncertainty about whether a situation is dangerous.

These guides are designed to help you prioritise distance, visibility, support, and calm first steps. They may suggest avoiding further contact, moving toward safer places, preserving evidence where safe, or contacting emergency or specialist support.

PanicStation.org provides general information only. It is not emergency help, legal advice, safeguarding or protective-services advice, counselling, advocacy, or professional safety planning.
