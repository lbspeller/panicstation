# Contact

You can contact PanicStation.org at:

support@panicstation.org

## What you can contact us about

You can use this address for:

- reporting an error in a guide;
- reporting outdated information;
- reporting a broken or irrelevant link;
- asking a privacy question;
- reporting an accessibility issue;
- sending general feedback about the site.

## Reporting a guide error

If you are reporting a problem with a guide, please include:

- the guide title;
- the page URL;
- the section or sentence you are concerned about;
- what you think is wrong, unclear, or outdated;
- a source or reference, if you have one.

Correction reports may lead to an update, clarification, source change, or removal of wording. We may not be able to reply to every message, but reports are reviewed where possible.

## What PanicStation.org cannot help with

PanicStation.org cannot provide personalised advice, emergency help, legal representation, medical care, counselling, casework, advocacy, or direct intervention in your situation.

If someone is in immediate danger, call the relevant emergency service for your location.

If your situation is serious, time-sensitive, or could affect your legal rights, health, housing, employment, money, safety, family, immigration status, or liberty, contact an appropriate qualified professional or specialist service.

## Sensitive information

Please do not send sensitive personal documents unless necessary.

PanicStation.org is an information website, not a casework or advice service.
